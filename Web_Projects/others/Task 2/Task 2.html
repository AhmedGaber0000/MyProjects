    This article is about the original company that existed from 1939 to 2015. Not the current company

    The Hewlett-Packard Company, usually abbreviated to HP, was an American multinational information technology company headquartered in Palo Alto , California , that developed and provided a variety of hardware components, as well as software and related services to consumers and small businesses. medium-sized ( SMBs ) and large corporations, including clients in the government, health and education sectors. The company was founded in a single-car garage of Hewlett-Packard company in Palo Alto,California , by Bill Hewlett and David Packard in 1939, initially producing a range of electronic test and measurement equipment. The Hewlett-Packard Garage at 367 Addison Avenue is now designated an official California Historic Landmark, marked by a plaque describing the place as " The Birthplace of Silicon Valley ".
